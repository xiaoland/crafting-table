# Mode D - Diagnose

## Role

Use when observed reality diverges from expectation and evidence must be collected before action.

## Guardrails

- No evidence, no modification.
- Do not collapse multiple hypotheses into one guess.

## Read-do steps

1. Capture the symptom, timeline, and blast radius.
2. Collect direct evidence.
3. Rank hypotheses by evidence quality and missing evidence.
4. Decide whether the next justified step is more diagnosis, exploration, or execution.

## Exit criteria

- Likely causes are ranked with evidence.
- Missing evidence is explicit.
- The next action is justified rather than guessed.
