  platform or dependency limit: the product is iPad-first and should use native SwiftUI patterns unless there is clear pressure otherwise.

- compliance or legal: none.
  budget or staffing: the first release must stay finishable.
  platform or dependency limit: the first remote shape is terminal-first with file transfer, not GUI remote desktop.

- compliance or legal: none.
  budget or staffing: trust and review surfaces cannot explode first-version scope.
