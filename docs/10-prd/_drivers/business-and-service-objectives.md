# Business and service objectives

## Objectives

- business objective: prove that `Xiaoland Workbench` can be personally useful on iPad in real repeat use.
  service objective: let the user orient, resume, capture, and act from one coherent workbench.
  success rationale: `0.1.0` should feel like one believable system rather than disconnected experiments.

- business objective: keep the first release human-centered and legible.
  service objective: avoid collapsing the product into an agent-centric or developer-only tool.
  success rationale: the broader shared human/agent workspace can grow later if the human-operated baseline is strong.

- business objective: make the first release small enough to finish.
  service objective: prove the core loop before broad integrations, advanced review, or autonomous restructuring.
  success rationale: a narrow release gives cleaner feedback than a feature pile.
